The square root of $h$ is equal to $3$ times
the square root of $k$.  If $h$ is equal to
twice $k$ plus $14$, what is the ordered pair
$(h, k)$?



\ifsat
	\begin{enumerate}[label=\Alph*)]
		\item $(2,18)$
		\item $(18,2)$%
		\item $(14,42)$
		\item There are no real solutions for $(h,k)$
	\end{enumerate}
\else
\fi
