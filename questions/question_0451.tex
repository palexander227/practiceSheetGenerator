


  Three vases are filled with flowers.  If the ratio of flowers in the three vases is $1:2:3$, then how many flowers can there be in total?


\ifsat
	\begin{enumerate}[label=\Alph*)]
		\item  $3$
		\item  $180$% 
		\item  $100$
		\item  $130$
	\end{enumerate}
\else
\fi
