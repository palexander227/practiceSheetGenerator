$$\frac{1}{f}=\frac{1}{d_a}+\frac{1}{d_r}$$
The equation above is used for describing the apparent distance of an image in a concave mirror ($d_a$) in relation to the real distance of the object ($d_r$) and the focal length of the mirror ($f$).  If the focal length of the mirror is constant, and the object is closer than the focal length, which of the following graphs accurately represents the apparent distance in the mirror as a function of the actual distance of the object?
